Твоя кар'єра починається з нами

23 квітня | DEC place

Це твій шанс знайти роботу своєї мрії

Kyiv Post Employment Fair is a biannual recruiting event that brings together leading companies and prospective employees. It has played its role as a career matchmaker for already 12 years. Employment Fair usually starts at 10 am and last till 4 pm. During this time, HRs meet with potential applicants to tell them about open positions and career opportunities at their companies. This year, recruiters will also have a chance to conduct on-the-spot job interviews with the most successful candidates.

Цього року на Ярмарку приймуть участь 19 компаній
